August 1 2021 - The ascend of prices and the market value of NFTs begins to increase, as the market price of the NFTs reach almost 2 Billion USD by the end of the month. This was the beginning of the NFT empire, that ruled the media for the next year. The top NFT collection, CryptoPunk, began selling for an average of 2.5 Million USD.
September 1 2021- The highest point for NFTs, where the market price was raised over 2 Billion USD, as NFT artworks began auctioning. The internet auction house of NFTs was established.
October 1 2021 - Following the general trend of anything hype based on the internet, NFTs became popular, then proceeded to die out. The NFT market experienced a recession of almost 1.5 Billion USD. As the market shrank and initiated the death and perpetual death of NFTs.
December 1 2021 - The down struck of NFTs, because of the mainstream attention from media and other outlets, mainly disapproved and despised the ever rising price of NFTs. This was especially true, during the time ‘Stan Lee’ , creator of the Marvel comics, was revived from the dead, to tweet his support of NFTs. This marked a lot of controversy, about the rise and use of NFTs especially those that were being monopolised by individuals and large corporations.
Current - The NFT marketplafce is still vibrant and active, as new and upcoming artists use NFTs as a way to promote their artworks, while getting paid. The idea of a personalised blockchain has also been taken into consideration for cybersecurity, as others begin looking for new ways to implement and use the NFT identity in the blockchain for more efficient and safer transactions of cryptocurrency.

INTRODUCTION

NFTs are a form of cryptocurrency built differently from regular coins and have boomed in popularity in 2021. These non-fungible tokens allow buyers online to have proof of ownership in a world where things are copied and pasted and people feign right of possession unless copyrighted.



Our survey was conducted in order to see what people’s thoughts are for NFTs. This meant targeting the general public who have a modest knowledge of the internet. Our questions are aimed at how people perceive NFTs as a trend and product as well as their existence economically as an investment, it asks questions about cryptocurrency first to establish a baseline before moving onto NFTs. The survey also informed respondents what cryptocurrency and NFTs were in case they were unsure about its details.

MAKING AND PURPOSE OF THE INFOGRAPHIC

For the survey and infographic, our team decided to discuss NFTs and cryptocurrency. We decided to research this topic in order to inform respondents as well as shed light on the opinions of NFTs and various cryptocurrencies. We also wanted to discuss relatively new concepts and gain an understanding of how people view them whether it may be positively or negatively. The infographic will be the main method to inform the audience about the team’s findings. Aiming to educate the audience on the concept of NFTs and cryptocurrencies along with people’s views and opinions on them. It will be targeted towards the general public who are somewhat versed in the internet and are aware of current technologies such as cryptocurrency. The primary data collected by our team will be the general opinion of cryptocurrency and NFTs and how people think of the impacts. Meanwhile, the secondary data collected will include the environmental and economic impact of NFTs due to it being out of our scope to conduct a survey.

Aside from asking for consent to display respondent answers, any data collected from the survey will be kept securely online with access only by a shared link and permission. CONCLUSION

EVALUATION OF DATA

The opinion of NFTs is somewhat different from cryptocurrency. While roughly half of the respondents would buy or invest in cryptocurrency, only 19% would buy an NFT. Furthermore, half of the respondents do not approve of the rapid growth of NFTs; this is when mentioning the environmental costs of NFT transactions. Overall, many people are familiar with cryptocurrency and NFTs. However, people would prefer to invest in cryptocurrency coins compared to NFTs.
